Understanding Bipolar
Bipolar disorder involves shifts in mood, energy, and activity levels that go beyond typical ups and downs. These states are not choices — they are part of a complex neurological condition.
Mood States
Mania
Racing thoughts, high energy, reduced sleep, impulsivity, or euphoria.
Hypomania
Elevated mood, good energy, more talkative and creative.
Stable
Balanced mood, normal energy, clear thinking.
Depression
Low mood, low energy, sleep changes, withdrawal.
Living With Bipolar
The symptoms lists don't always capture the full picture. These are some of the quieter realities people live with every day.
